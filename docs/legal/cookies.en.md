# Cookie Policy

> **The short version:** Vout only uses technical cookies (to keep your session) and preference cookies (to remember light/dark theme). **No advertising trackers, no third-party analytics.** You'll be able to review your cookie preferences from the footer link.

> ⚠️ This page is an early draft. Final content, along with the consent banner and preferences panel, arrives in the next phase of the legal plan.

## 1. What a cookie is

A cookie is a small file stored in your browser to remember information across visits. `localStorage` and `sessionStorage` are similar technologies that are legally treated the same way.

## 2. Cookies we use

| Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `vout-session` | Technical | Keep your session logged in | 120 minutes of inactivity |
| `XSRF-TOKEN` | Technical | CSRF form protection | Session |
| `remember_web_*` | Technical | Keep you signed in if you ticked "remember me" | 5 years |
| `appearance` | Preference | Remember your light/dark/system theme | 1 year |
| `sidebar_state` | Preference | Remember sidebar open/closed state | 1 year |
| `appearance` (localStorage) | Preference | Local theme mirror to prevent flash on load | Until browser is cleared |

They are all **first-party** and **exempt from prior consent** under Article 22.2 LSSI-CE, being strictly necessary or user preferences.

## 3. How to manage them

A preferences panel will land in the footer soon. Meanwhile you can:

- Delete all cookies from your browser settings.
- Block specific cookies in your browser's privacy settings.
- Reject them before they are written by browsing in private/incognito mode.

## 4. Third parties

Today Vout does **not share** cookies with third parties. If someday we add services that require them (e.g., a self-hosted analytics provider), we will request prior consent via banner and update this table.

## 5. Changes

If we add, remove or change the use of any cookie, we'll update this page and the banner will reopen on your next visit.
