## 1. Purpose

These terms govern the use of the Vout portal ({{domain}}) and its associated identity service (IdP). By creating an account or using the portal, you accept these terms.

## 2. Your account

- You must be **at least 14 years old** (digital consent age in Spain).
- The data you provide at sign-up must be accurate.
- You are responsible for keeping your password safe. We recommend enabling 2FA at `Settings → Two-factor authentication`.
- You can delete your account anytime at `Settings → Privacy`.

## 3. Acceptable conduct

Please **don't**:

- Abuse the identity service (massive login attempts, brute force, scraping the games catalog).
- Try to access other people's accounts or exploit vulnerabilities without prior authorization.
- Upload illegal content to the catalog if acting as a developer.
- Use Vout to impersonate another person or brand.

Security vulnerabilities should be reported responsibly to {{contact_email}} — please don't disclose them publicly before we can patch them.

## 4. Embedded third-party games

When you play a title published by an external developer:

- **Game content, bugs and support are the developer's responsibility**, not Vout's.
- Vout hosts the game inside an iframe and, if requested and you authorize it, hands an OAuth token with the permissions you approve.
- You can **revoke access for any app** at `Settings → Authorized applications`.

## 5. Gesture controls (MediaPipe)

Facial control is **optional**. You activate it explicitly, it's processed in your browser, and you can disable it at any time without losing your account.

If you use the feature:

- The camera only turns on when you ask for it.
- We never send images to any server.
- You can recalibrate or reset your gesture profile at `Settings → Appearance`.

## 6. OAuth ecosystem and connected apps

If you integrate your Vout identity with an external app:

- Authorizing it is your decision: on first use you see exactly what data it's asking for.
- You can revoke access anytime.
- Vout is not responsible for third-party apps. If an app misbehaves, report it to {{contact_email}}.

In all cases we'll notify you by email unless urgency or law prevents it.
