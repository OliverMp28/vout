## 2. What Vout is

Vout is two things at once:

1. **A browser mini-games portal** — explore and play games directly in your browser, no downloads.
2. **An Identity Provider (IdP)** based on OAuth 2.0 — you can reuse your Vout account to sign in to other apps that integrate the ecosystem (e.g., games published by third-party developers). You decide which apps can connect and what permissions you grant.

Vout also ships **computer-vision technology (MediaPipe)** for facial-gesture controls in supported games. This feature is **always optional**, activated explicitly by the user, and runs entirely inside your browser — camera frames are never sent to any server.
